About Me

My family has several "computer types." I have always been fascinated with computers. My mother was a computer programmer in the days before the PC, programming mainframe computers that used punch cards for input! My son took web development in high school and his team won tournaments. He has worked in IT for over 10 years and is currently a systems administrator. My nephew is the CEO of a web development company, having been a student in web development and business management.


I took my first computer course consisting of Cobol and RPG2 when I was 18 years old. Later, I took online courses in html, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap.


Having worked as a paralegal for a number of years, I decided to change career paths. I wanted to expand my web development skills and put them to good use. I enrolled in a Full Stack Web Development course through the University of Denver. As a recent graduate of the program, I am ready to begin my journey in this very interesting field.
